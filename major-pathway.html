Your Path to a Health Science Degree

Program Duration

Total Time: 4 years (120-128 credit hours)

Full-time Status: 15-16 credits per semester

Part-time Options: Available with extended completion time

Academic Plan by Year

First Year

  • General Biology
  • Chemistry
  • English Composition
  • Mathematics
  • Introduction to Health Science

Second Year

  • Anatomy & Physiology
  • Medical Terminology
  • Statistics
  • Psychology
  • Healthcare Ethics

Third Year

  • Research Methods
  • Health Policy
  • Epidemiology
  • Healthcare Management
  • Clinical Practice

Fourth Year

  • Advanced Health Science
  • Internship
  • Capstone Project
  • Specialization Courses
  • Professional Development

Estimated Costs

Expense Estimated Cost
Tuition (per year) $25,000 - $35,000
Books and Supplies $1,200 - $1,800
Lab Fees $500 - $1,000
Clinical Experience Fees $300 - $800

Note: Costs may vary by institution and location. Financial aid and scholarships are available.
