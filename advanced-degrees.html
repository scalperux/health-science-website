Advanced Degrees in Health Science

Master's Degree Programs

Master of Public Health (MPH)

Duration: 2 years

Focus: Population health, epidemiology, health policy

Perfect for those interested in public health initiatives and policy-making.

Master of Health Administration (MHA)

Duration: 2-3 years

Focus: Healthcare management, leadership, operations

Ideal for future healthcare administrators and executives.

Master of Science in Health Science (MSHS)

Duration: 1.5-2 years

Focus: Clinical research, health informatics, education

Suitable for those pursuing research or specialized clinical roles.

Doctoral Programs

Ph.D. in Health Sciences

Duration: 4-6 years

Focus: Research methodology, advanced theory

Designed for future researchers and university professors.

Doctor of Health Science (DHSc)

Duration: 3-5 years

Focus: Advanced practice, leadership

Perfect for practitioners seeking leadership positions.

Admission Requirements

Master's Programs

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Minimum GPA (usually 3.0)
  • GRE scores
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose

Doctoral Programs

  • Master's degree (sometimes required)
  • Research experience
  • Higher GPA requirements
  • Professional experience
  • Research proposal

Funding Options

  • Graduate Assistantships
  • Research Fellowships
  • Federal Grants
  • Employer Tuition Assistance
